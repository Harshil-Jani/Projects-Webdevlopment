Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs Founder of Apple inc. and creator of Sarif and Sans-serif Fonts.

  • Steve Jobs was American Business Magnate, Industrial Designer, Inverstor and media proprietor.
  • He was CEO, Chairman and Co-Founder of Apple inc. and Pixar, and Board of Directory Member of The Walt Disney Company.
  • Jobs and Steve Wozniak founded Apple in 1976 to sell Wozniak's Apple I personal Computer.
  • Steve Jobs was a Dropout from Stanford University.
  • He made Macintosh which was the first ever successful mass-market Personal Computer to have featured a graphical user Interface, Built-In screen and Mouse.
